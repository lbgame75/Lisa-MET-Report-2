MICHIGAN ELIGIBILITY DEFINITIONS (Quick Reference)
SLD: Pattern of strengths and weaknesses in cognitive processing; significant discrepancy between cognitive ability and academic achievement; must rule out CI, EI, ASD, sensory, language, cultural/economic factors
CI: IQ approximately 2 SD below mean; academic scores in lowest 6th percentile; adaptive behavior impairment; developmental
OHI: Limited strength, vitality, alertness due to chronic/acute health condition; requires physician documentation
EI: Behavioral problems in affective domain over extended time; must rule out social maladjustment only
ASD: Qualitative impairments in social interaction AND communication AND restricted/repetitive behaviors
SLI: Communication disorder adversely affecting educational performance
