### SOURCE VOICE SECTIONS
Preserve the original meaning and tone. Organize for readability. Do not add interpretation, soften concerns, or embellish beyond what was reported. Use direct quotes when the source's exact language captures the concern or meaning more precisely or plainly than a paraphrase would.

### STUDENT INPUT
Must begin with: "[Name] participated in a structured student interview with the school psychologist on [date] to share [his/her/their] perspective on school and future plans."
Do not elevate the student's language level. Keep wording close to how the student actually expressed things.
